WELCOME TO CASA DE AMOR

Welcome to Casa de Amor Mexican Bar & Kitchen Formerly known as Tacos Mexican Restaurant, we have been serving authentic Mexican flavors in Hobart since 1977. Now reborn as Casa de Amor, we continue our passion for fresh, handcrafted food made from scratch, celebrating the rich traditions of Mexican cuisine. Nestled in the historic Gibsons Mill, our cozy and vibrant space brings together culture, flavor, and a touch of romance. Whether you're here for a fiesta with friends or a quiet moment with a margarita, we invite you to the taste of Mexico, made with love. Bienvenidos & enjoy!

THE BAR & KITCHEN

Welcome to Casa de Amor, the home of damn good Mexican food and ridiculously fun vibes in Hobart since 1977.

We’re talking tacos that slap, burritos that hit the spot, and margaritas so big they’ve become Hobart legend. From long lunches and date nights to cheeky cocktails with your crew, Casa is where flavour meets fiesta

Pull up a seat in our colourful bar, sip on our famous fishbowl margaritas, or dive into a plate of nachos that’s definitely not made for sharing (but we won’t stop you).

Whether you’re craving the best Mexican food in Hobart, planning a group night out, or just hunting for a spicy new go to bar - Casa’s got you.

ATRIO - Hobart’s Newest All-Day Bar & Kitchen

Say hola to Atrio, the cheeky little sibling of Casa de Amor – serving morning coffees, long brunches, and late-night tapas right in the heart of Hobart.

By day, Atrio is your sunny spot for smooth espresso, fresh bakes, and slow brunches. By night, it flips into a moody, intimate bar with killer cocktails, Latin-style small plates, and a soundtrack that makes you want to stay a little longer (and then a little longer after that).

Whether you're kicking off your morning or kicking on till midnight, Atrio blends relaxed charm with just the right amount of spice.

Come for the coffee. Stay for the cocktails.

Atrio – Hobart’s new favourite hangout.

OUR HOUSE HEROES

Behind every taco, cocktail, and smile is a team of legends. They’re the early starters, the late finishers, the spice masters, the plate runners, and the mood makers.

From the heat of the kitchen to the rhythm of the floor, they bring passion, pride, and pure hospitality every single day.

We’re proud of our team. We trust them with our casa. And we know without them, none of this would taste the same.
