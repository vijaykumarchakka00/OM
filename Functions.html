FUNCTION ROOM HIRE PRAHRAN

At Death or Glory, we offer unrivalled function spaces designed to make your celebrations even more memorable with unique themed rooms, and stellar food and drinks packages, all without venue hire fees, only a minimum spend.

The Island Bar is a secluded tropical oasis accommodating up to 40 guests, perfect for intimate gatherings. And our premium Saloon space, hosts up to 60 guests, boasting a private bar, stage, and versatile amenities for exhibitions, live music, or exclusive events.

From birthdays, work parties, bucks/hens nights to private functions.. We'll make your event a night to remember!

THE ISLAND BAR

The Island Bar is our hidden retreat that instantly transports you from the hustle and bustle of busy Chapel Street just downstairs, to a tropical island oasis getaway.

Ideal for up to 40 guests, the Island Bar is capable of hosting a range of events such as birthdays, work functions, bucks and hens parties and sit down meals — making it perfectly suited for almost any event, where both privacy and intimacy is of utmost importance.

There are no venue hire fees associated with hiring the Island Bar — simply a minimum spend, which can include both your food and drink spend.

TESTIMONIALS

Nigel Fordham

Sickest bar, great staff and awesome atmosphere. A must see destination. They serve amazing looking cocktails and food. Sports is always playing on the big screen and they also have a pool table for you and your mates. If it gets cold they fire up the gas heaters to ensure a positive customer experience. World class. Highly recommended.

Emma Murphy

We went to this place amid all the current covid restrictions and the staff could not have been more accommodating. They dealt with a large group, made food and drink accommodations and gave us amazing service. We’ve been to a couple places since the new restrictions came into place and this was by far the most pleasant experience.

Hanna Burrows
